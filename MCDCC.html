Who am I?

My name is Nathan Feldman, and I represent you on the Montgomery County Democratic Central Committee in district 15.

I grew up in Pikesville's Jewish community and learned the values of solidarity and justice from my upbringing.

I graduated from the University of Maryland with a Bachelor's in mathematics and a Master's in public policy.

Now I want to use my experience to make Maryland even more BLUE!

Get out the vote!

The Democratic Central Committee is the most important county-wide voter outreach organization.

We need to keep Democrats in office everywhere from the White House to our County Council - and I have more than four years of experience in voter outreach working to do just that.

Fundraise for Democrats!

As your representative, I am empowered to fundraise for Democrats to protect the progress we made in 2020 and 2022.

We must retake our majorities in Congress.

I'll be sure to not only help local candidates but also swing state Senate candidates in Georgia and Pennsylvania.

Appointments to Annapolis...

Members of the MCDCC have the responsibility to appoint members of the General Assembly in Annapolis to fill vacancies left by Democrats.

Unfortunately, the Maryland state constitution does not provide for special elections to fill legislative vacancies.

My number one priority in filling vacancies: finding appointees who will protect and expand reproductive health services in the state of Maryland.
